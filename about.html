About Us

We’re a brother and sister duo who grew up running through the lush fields of La Fortuna de San Carlos, Costa Rica, with the majestic Arenal Volcano watching over us. From an early age, nature, well-being, and adventure became part of who we are.

Our deep love for our hometown shaped our appreciation for the land, and later, Nosara stole our hearts with its vibrant energy and peaceful spirit. What started as a personal escape turned into a calling: to help others experience the magic of this incredible place.

As locals and nature lovers, we’re slowly building Nosara Adventures to share authentic, enriching experiences.
