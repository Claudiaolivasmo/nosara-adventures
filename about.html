About Us

We're a brother and sister duo who grew up running through the green pastures of La Fortuna de San Carlos, Costa Rica, always under the watchful gaze of the majestic Arenal Volcano. From a young age, nature, adventure, and well-being have been at the heart of our lives.

Our love for our hometown shaped our deep respect for the land. Later, Nosara captivated us with its unique energy and peaceful rhythm. What began as a personal escape soon became a mission: to help others experience the soul of this magical place.
